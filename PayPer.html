Why use PayPer?

Shop for the best worldwide

Over 10 million online stores across more than 200 markets accept PayPer. Buy quality good from international stores right from your home. Once you spot a great deal, just click the PayPer button and buy with confidence.

Your purchases are protected

With our 24/7 transaction monitoring, anti-fraud technologies and Buyer Protection, you can shop with peace of mind.

Your return shipping is on us

You have one less thing to worry, even when you change your mind about your purchases. Simply ship the item back to the seller and get a refund of up to Php 1000 on the return shipping cost through our Refunded Returns service.

We work with major banks in Philippines and overseas, so you can pay with your preferred credit or debit card and continue earning reward points*.
